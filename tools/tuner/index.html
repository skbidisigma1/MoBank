How to use:

  1. Click "Start Tuner" to begin
  2. Allow microphone access when prompted
  3. Play a note on your instrument
  4. Adjust your instrument until the indicator shows it's in tune (centered at 0¢)
  5. Green indicator means your note is in tune (±5¢)
  6. Yellow indicator means your note is slightly off (±15¢)
  7. Red indicator means your note needs adjustment (> ±15¢)

This tuner uses the Pitchy library for accurate pitch detection.
